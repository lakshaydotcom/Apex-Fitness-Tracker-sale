🚀 Chitkara University · BE 2025
Train Smarter,
Achieve More
APEX is a complete fitness tracking platform combining workout logging, smart nutrition analysis, body weight monitoring, and an AI-powered coach connected to Claude AI (Anthropic) for personalised, intelligent coaching.
🎯
Our Objective

APEX was built to make elite fitness tracking accessible to everyone. By keeping workouts, nutrition, and insights in one place, you identify patterns, break plateaus, and hit goals faster.

🏗️
Tech Stack

Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — zero frameworks, zero dependencies. Data is stored in localStorage per user. AI Coach powered by Claude AI (Anthropic). Nutrition powered by Open Food Facts global database. Visitor count via CounterAPI.

Features
🔐
User Auth
Multi-user login system with separate data per account.
🎨
4 Themes
Dark, Light, Ocean, Forest — switch any time from the dot panel.
🏋️
Workout Log
Log exercises with sets, reps, weight. Auto-estimates calories and tracks PRs.
🥗
Nutrition
Built-in food database — type any food to get instant macro breakdowns.
🤖
AI Coach
Real Claude AI coach reads your data and gives personalised advice.
💪
Body Type Plans
Ectomorph, Mesomorph, Endomorph — each gets a specific exercise plan.
